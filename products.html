Pricing

Due to contracts, broadcasting of the race on national television, and other sponsorships, the round trip will cost only $500,000 per person to compete. A reward of $1,000,000 is given to first place, and the runner-up gets $750,000. Because all contestants must stay for the duration due to transportation costs, contestants will continue to appear within the Great Space Race in non-competing roles. Please understand the Great Space Race may decline your application, even if you meet all requirements as listed within the document.
